其中XXX的值为kkk的概率记作b(k;n;p)b(k;n;p)b(k;n;p)

b(k;n;p)=(nk)pk(1−p)n−kb(k;n;p) = {n \choose k} p^k(1-p)^{n-k} b(k;n;p)=(kn​)pk(1−p)n−k

E(X)=npD(X)=np(1−p)\begin{aligned} E(X) &= np\\ D(X) &= np(1-p) \end{aligned} E(X)D(X)​=np=np(1−p)​
